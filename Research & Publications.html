Undergraduate Thesis

Institution: Department of Mechanical Engineering, BUET, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Topic: A Numerical Simulation on the Effect of Variation of Fuel Injection Pressure on Air-fuel Mixing in a Dual-cavity Scramjet Combustor.

Supervisor: Dr. Mohammad Ali, Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering, BUET.

  • The numerical simulation was performed using the finite volume method in ANSYS FLUENT 22.0.
  • The flow field and shockwaves were analyzed by generating numerical schlieren images in Tec Plot 360 EX 2021 R2.
  • Fuel injection pressures and cavity aspect ratios were taken as variable parameters and their effects on the performance parameters of a hypersonic vehicle were explicitly evaluated.

Technical Report

Institution: Department of Mechanical Engineering, BUET, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Topic: Experimental and Numerical Study on Internal Forced Convection Heat Transfer in a Horizontal Tube. [Full Report]

Authors: Monoranjan Debnath Rony, Hasan Tareq Mahin, Saiful Islam, Syed Tahmid Kalam, MD. Mahbub Talukder.

  • The numerical simulation was performed using COMSOL Multiphysics.
  • Finite Element Method was used for simulation.
  • The performance of the tube was also evaluated by analyzing various fluid properties and parameters through simulation.
